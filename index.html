About Social Security Disability Benefits

Social Security Disability Insurance (SSDI) provides financial support to people who are unable to work due to a long-term medical condition. Qualifying conditions must meet strict criteria established by the Social Security Administration (SSA).

Eligibility Criteria

  • You must have a medical condition that is expected to last at least 12 months.
  • You must have worked long enough and recently enough under Social Security.
  • Your medical condition must prevent you from performing substantial work.
